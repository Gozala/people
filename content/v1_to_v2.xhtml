Firefox Contacts: Upgraded to version 0.2

Firefox Contacts has been updated to version 0.2.

As part of this update, your contact database has been reset.

Click here to open the Contacts management screen and reactivate your contacts.

Please visit our site if you have any comments or feedback.


Release Notes:

  • Contact services are now added with a "connect" button; they can be refreshed or disconnected independently of other services.
  • Database changes to support per-service refresh and disconnect. These changes require a reset of the contact database.
  • New service connectors for LinkedIn and Plaxo.
  • A new web discovery button on each contact detail page searches public websites to find more information about a contact.
  • International character import is now correct on MacOS.
